Notes on HTML Tags

Index

Here are a few HTML tags I have recently learned:

< p >
The paragraph tag is used to define and separate paragraphs on a page.

< em >
The emphasis tag is used to stress the importance of a string of text.

< strong >
The strong tag is used to stress the importance of much more important text.

< hr >
The horizontal rule tag places a horizontal line on the page to add a break or separate content.

< a >
The anchor tag is used to link content to the page by adding locations with attributes.
