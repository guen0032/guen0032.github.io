My name is Charlie, I am 28 years old. I am originally from the small town of Kingston, Nova Scotia. My family moved to the United Kingdom when I was 10. We then moved to Winnipeg, where we spent a few years before coming to Ottawa. I have travelled to many other countries, most recently to Mexico to see the Mayan Riviera. My partner and I had a trip planned for Cuba in 2020 but unfortunately Covid had other plans.

Having worked in both food service and in retail, I am looking to make a change in career paths. I enrolled in the Interactive Media Design program to learn a variety of new skills.
