About Me

Hello! Welcome to my page!

My name is Charlie, I am 28 years old. I am originally from the small town of Kingston, Nova Scotia. My family moved to the United Kingdom after my 9th birthday. A year later we moved to Winnipeg, where we spent a few years before coming to Ottawa. I enjoy travel, and visiting other countries. Most recently, I went to Mexico with my partner and his family. We stayed in the Mayan Riviera near Tulum, where I hope to one day visit again.

Having worked in both food service and in retail, I am looking to make a change in career paths. I enrolled in the IMD program to learn a variety of new skills and hopefully one day become my own boss. When I am not doing school work I am constantly listening to music. My favourite genre is house and tech-house. Lately I have been listening to a lot of Martin Ikin and Hannah Wants. In my spare time I am a hobbyist DJ and also enjoy playing the guitar. During this program I am most looking forward to learning about digital graphics and web development.
